Connect a Google Sheet

Paste your client's published CSV link. The dashboard builds itself and stays live.

1
Make sure the Google Sheet is either Published to web (File → Share → Publish to web → CSV) or shared as "Anyone with link can view"
2
Paste any URL from that sheet below — the edit link, the published link, anything. The dashboard will auto-detect the right format.
3
To link from your management sheet, add ?sheet=URL to this page's address
💡 Tip: In your management sheet, add a column with this formula to create one-click dashboard links:
=HYPERLINK("https://YOURDOMAIN/index.html?sheet="&ENCODEURL(B2),"📊 Dashboard")
